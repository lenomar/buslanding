Game Removal Guidelines

To remove an already listed game, please apply according to the specifications:

Removal email:bus@buslanding.com
Email subject: [Game Removal] Game Name

Email template:

Game Name: Full name of the game to be removed Removal Reason: Please explain in detail (copyright issues/content violations etc.) Supporting Documents: Such as copyright proof (can be attached) Applicant Identity: Need to prove relationship to the game (developer/copyright holder etc.) Contact Info: Your email/QQ/WeChat

Important Notes:

  • Non-copyright holders need to provide sufficient removal reasons
  • Fraudulent applications will result in permanent email blacklisting
  • Processing time: 1-3 business days

Frequently Asked Questions

A: Approved games will go live within 1-2 business days, extended during major holidays.

Q: Why was my game not approved?

A: Common reasons include: content violations, inaccessible links, incomplete descriptions, etc. We will email you the specific reason.
